Frequently Asked Questions

What are your audience demographics?

Our audience is primarily 18-34 years old (73%), with a 65% male and 35% female split. They are tech-savvy, early adopters, and primarily from the US (60%), UK (12%), Canada (8%), Australia (5%), and other regions (15%).

What ad formats do you support?

We support standard IAB units including leaderboards (728×90), medium rectangles (300×250), mobile banners (320×50), as well as custom formats like skin takeovers, interstitials, and native advertising opportunities.

Do you offer sponsored content?

Yes! Our team of experienced gaming writers can create sponsored articles, reviews, and features that align with your marketing goals while resonating with our audience. All sponsored content is clearly marked as such for transparency.

How do you track campaign performance?

We provide comprehensive analytics including impressions, clicks, CTR, engagement metrics, and demographic data. For premium packages, we offer more detailed reporting including heat maps, scroll depth, and time-on-page metrics.

What is your content approval process?

All advertising content must be submitted at least 3 business days before the campaign start date. Our team will review materials for compliance with our guidelines. We may suggest changes to improve performance or ensure compliance with our standards.
